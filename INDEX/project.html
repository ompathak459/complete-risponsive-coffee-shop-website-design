about us

what makes our coffee special

We make our own coffee special by the way we make it, the ingredients we use, and through the rituals we associate with it. Coffee is much more to coffee lovers than a mere relaxing beverage or a quick energy drink.

Many would argue that for coffee to be truly special it needs to satisfy multiple requirements, and only a real balance between where the beans are sourced, how they are roasted, and last but not least how the coffee is brewed results in a cup of truly special coffee.
